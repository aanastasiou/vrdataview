Welcome

Two brief demos of Swansea University and the work that is taking place there are provided through this page.

The first, is a brief demonstration of Swansea University and the surrounding area of outstanding physical beauty.

The second is a two part demonstration of embedding a viewer inside a dataset via an immersive VR experience. Two datasets are provided at two different levels of complexity:

Fisher's Iris Flower Dataset

The original dataset used by Sir Ronald Fisher to demonstrate the technique of Linear Discriminant Analysis and obtained via the UCI machine learning repository.

Due to its simplicity, the dataset is often used in Machine Learning to demonstrate various techniques.

In this setting, the 4 original features where reduced to 2 following a standard feature selection process and then these two features where mapped on a cylindrical space to clearly demonstrate the shape of the three classes they describe.

Breast Cancer Wisconsin

The dataset used by Wolberg et al in disciminating breast cancer cases as obtained via the UCI machine learning repository.

In this setting, the 30 original features where reduced to 10 following a standard feature selection process and then these 10 features where re-mapped to just 3 using Multidimensional Scaling in an attempt to demonstrate the shape of the two classes (malignant, benign) they describe.

This is a slightly more complicated dataset and includes three blue pointers for re-positioning the camera to different places within a dataset.
